About

Write CY is a platform for community creative writing and storytelling founded in 2015 by Max Sheridan and Marilena Zackheos. We're based in Nicosia, Cyprus.

Site info: This site is a few HTML files hosted on GitHub. Body copy is Manifold Sans, headings are Plastic Script, both by HEX. Audio player is by Weird Bubba Studio. Design and art are by Tug Wells.
